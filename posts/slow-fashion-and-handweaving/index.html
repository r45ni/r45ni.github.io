Slow Fashion and Handweaving

Wearing your baby, or wearing stylish functional accessories? Ladida is the whimsical, easygoing approach to handwoven garments. With an asterisk for explorations in hand spinning, knitting, dyeing and other fiber arts.

Visit the Facebook group or page, or follow the Instagram account to see what is happening on the loom, in the dye pot, at the spinning wheel or the finishing table.
